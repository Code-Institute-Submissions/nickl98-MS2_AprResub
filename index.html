How to Play?

  • Step 1 : Click Play Game at the top right of the page.
  • Step 2 : Click Start and the time will start counting down.
  • Step 3 : You have 100 seconds to match all the cards with the correct card!
  • Hint- Each time you start the game, the cards are shuffled
